    • Tamara Dimov, "Angel", 2021, Digital

    This piece is a drawing of a cherub that I had done digitally, and is apart of a larger scale image that is going to be a tattoo. This is in black and white, referenced from a photo of two cherubs, and hand shaded to give the piece a feel that it was drawn on paper. Due to some errors, this cherub was erased from my laptop along with multiple other pieces, but I do intend on redrawing this cherub again, and return him to his natural glory.
